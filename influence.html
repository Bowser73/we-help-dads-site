Program

Influence

Understanding that no one can change anyone else, our classroom programs provide guidance, information and testimonials to influence the dads to make the life changes needed to be a dad to their kids.

How It Works

The Influence program begins with classroom sessions where fathers are exposed to guidance, information, and real testimonials from men who have successfully made changes in their lives.

Rather than lecturing about what needs to change, our approach focuses on providing the information and inspiration that naturally influences dads to make the right choices for their families.

Program Topics

  • Understanding fatherhood responsibility
  • Emotional health and communication
  • Financial responsibility
  • Substance abuse awareness
  • Real stories from fathers who've made changes
