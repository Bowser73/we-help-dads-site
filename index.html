Who We Are

Strong dads build strong families.

We Help Dads is the public face of Dedicated Dads, an organization built around a simple belief: every dad deserves support, connection, and a community that has his back.

We exist for fathers and father figures at every stage — new dads, returning dads, stepdads, grandfathers, and the men who show up for the children in their lives. Through practical support and genuine community relationships, we help dads stay present for their families and connected to the people and resources around them.

What We Do

Three ways we show up for dads.

Fatherhood Connection

Creating space for dads to connect with one another — because no father should feel like he's doing this alone.

Family Strength

Encouraging the habits, presence, and relationships that help families grow closer and more resilient.

Resources & Referrals

Helping dads find their way to the right people, programs, and community resources when they need a hand.

Why It Matters

When dads are supported, everyone wins.

A supported dad is a more present dad. When fathers feel connected and equipped, their children feel it, their families feel it, and their communities feel it. Strengthening dads is one of the most direct ways to strengthen the people around them.
