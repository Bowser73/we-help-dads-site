Program

Help

When the men demonstrate a commitment to fatherhood and are free of any substance abuse, we help them connect and build their role as dads through legal assistance, job placement, social services coordination, and continuing mentoring.

Practical, Ongoing Support

For fathers who have demonstrated commitment to their transformation, we provide concrete, practical support. We understand that good intentions aren't enough—fathers need real help with real obstacles.

Our Help program removes barriers to fatherhood by providing legal assistance, employment support, and connections to community resources, paired with continuing mentoring from leaders who believe in their journey.

Services & Support

  • Legal assistance with custody and family matters
  • Job placement and employment support
  • Connections to social services and community resources
  • Ongoing mentoring and accountability
  • Community connection and belonging

Commitment & Results: Fathers in our Help program have demonstrated they're ready for change and substance-free living. We walk alongside them through the challenges of rebuilding their lives and their relationships with their children.
