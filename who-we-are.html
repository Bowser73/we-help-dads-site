Who We Are

The Board of Directors and Program Advisors who lead the mission to help fathers reconnect, grow, and stay involved.

Board of Directors

Jeffrey Hutter

Founder & President

Mr. Hutter retired from a career in dental and health care management in 2019 to begin a program that would reconnect fathers with their children—what is now We Help Dads. He was active in programs to mentor boys without fathers in Louisville and Kansas City. He began his career as a broadcast journalist after graduating from the University of Kansas William Allen White School of Journalism. He has a son and several step children and grandchildren with whom he is close in Indiana, Kentucky, and Texas, and an informally adopted son in Kentucky.

Ryan Farley

Secretary

Ryan is a dedicated addiction recovery professional with over 6 years of experience in mental health and substance use treatment.

He has a strong background in program development, mentorship, and community outreach, with a passion for breaking the stigma surrounding addiction and promoting recovery.

As a full-time father of two, Ryan leads with purpose, balancing his professional work with his most important role—being present for his children every day.

He is committed to helping individuals and families find hope, build resilience, and create lasting change through support, education, and second chances.
