Program

Prepare

Most of the men we help don't have a basis of fatherhood in their lives—there wasn't a dad to teach them. So we prepare the men to be a dad to their children through instruction and guest speakers sharing information from national fatherhood programs…and then measure what the men have gained from the classes.

Building a Foundation

For many fathers, there was no positive role model growing up. The Prepare program fills that gap by providing structured instruction and mentoring based on proven national fatherhood programs.

Through classroom sessions and guest speakers, fathers gain practical knowledge about parenting, child development, communication, and building healthy family relationships.

Learning Areas

  • Child development and behavior
  • Communication with children
  • Co-parenting and custody issues
  • Time management and priorities
  • National fatherhood best practices

Measurement & Progress: We track what participants learn and how they grow through the program, ensuring they're gaining the skills and knowledge needed for success.
