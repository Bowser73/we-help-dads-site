Community

Partner Programs

We work with courts, agencies, and community organizations to expand our reach and help more fathers reconnect with their families.

Problem Solving Courts

We collaborate with problem-solving courts to provide support and resources to fathers involved in the justice system who are working toward family reunification and personal growth.

TOWER Program

Partnership with the TOWER program to support fathers in workforce development, job training, and employment placement.

Madison County Corrections & Community Justice

We work with Madison County's correctional and community justice systems to provide support for fathers involved in the criminal justice process.

Indiana Supreme Court Legal Assistance

We Help Dads participates in Indiana Supreme Court-supported legal assistance initiatives to help fathers navigate family law matters and maintain meaningful relationships with their children.

Community Organizations & Churches

We collaborate with local churches, nonprofits, and community organizations to identify fathers who could benefit from our programs and to provide referral pathways into Dedicated Dads.
