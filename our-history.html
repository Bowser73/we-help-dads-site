History

The We Help Dads Story

We Help Dads was founded with a simple belief: fathers deserve support, encouragement, and a community that believes showing up matters. Beginning with Big Brother volunteering in 1975, the journey has grown into the comprehensive Dedicated Dads program.

Through classroom programs, mentoring, legal assistance, and job placement support, We Help Dads has worked with hundreds of fathers to help them reconnect with their children and build stronger families.
