Partner Programs

We work with courts, agencies, and community organizations to expand our reach and help more fathers reconnect with their families.

Problem Solving Court of Madison County

We work with the Problem Solving Court system to help dads involved in the justice system reconnect with their families and build toward successful reentry and family reunification.

Problem Solving Court of Grant County

Expanding our reach to support fathers in the Grant County Problem Solving Court system through our proven classroom and mentoring programs.

TOWER Program - Hamilton County Corrections

We provide fatherhood instruction in the Hamilton County Jail through our partnership with the TOWER Program, helping incarcerated fathers prepare for successful reentry and family connection.

Madison County Correction Department

We Help Dads began conducting classes in the Madison County Corrections Complex, bringing our programs to the correction department system.

Madison County Community Justice Department

We partner with the Community Justice Department to identify and support dads seeking to reconnect with their families and build stronger futures.

Indiana Supreme Court Legal Assistance Initiative

We Help Dads is the coordinator for Madison County Family Court of a grant from the Indiana Supreme Court to provide free legal assistance to parents referred by agencies seeking expanded rights with their children.

Community Organizations & Churches

We collaborate with local churches, nonprofits, and community organizations to identify fathers who could benefit from our programs and to provide referral pathways into Dedicated Dads.
